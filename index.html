What is idemeum?

idemeum is your digital identity

idemeum turns your mobile device into personal universal digital identity. It eliminates passwords, protects you against identity fraud, and enables 100% privacy.

How does it work?

We do things differently

We are passionate about making digital identity radically better. Meet Decentralzied, Verified, Mobile Identity Platform.

Decentralized

All user information is stored safely and securely on users' mobile devices. We store zero PII information in our backend.

idemeum is private by design.

Verified

When users create their identity, we remotely verify all information, such as email address, phone number, and ID document..

We reduce identity fraud and increase online trust.

Mobile

Mobile device replaces passwords and becomes universal digital identity. To access resources users leverage cryptography and biometric sensors on their devices.

We simplify user experience.
