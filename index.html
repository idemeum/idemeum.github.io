What is idemeum?

Decentralized Verified Mobile Identity Platform

We are passionate about making digital identity radically better. To do that we designed Decentralized, Verified, Mobile Digital Identity Platform.

Architecture

Decentralized

idemeum gives people power to own and control their digital identity.

We make identity secure and private.

Complete identity privacy

With digital identity we traded off privacy - our data is stored across myriad of centralized databases, and every authentication using social login is tracked and analyzed. That's why we designed idemeumt to be decentralized. idemeum stores zero personally identifiable information in the backend.

Every interaction is trusted

Identity fraud is at an all time high - billions in financial losses every year, and devastating consequences of identity theft for people's lives. That's why we designed idemeum to have all identity claims verified. idemeum makes every login and every interaction trusted.

Trust

Verified

idemeum verifies all user information to ensure that the user is who he says he is.

We reduce identity fraud.
Authentication

Mobile

Mobile device is your multifactor FIDO2 based authenticator.

We eliminate passwords.

Strong passwordless authentication

We keep relying on passwords that never protect us - billions of accounts compromised and released every year. That's why we designed idemeum to leverage modern authentication protocols and use mobile device as authenticator. idemeum removes passwords from the equation - your phone is your digital identity.
