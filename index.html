idemeum

Doing digital identity the right way

We built idemeum to make digital identity radically better. At the heart of our platform we have 3 guiding principles: Simplicity, Security, and Privacy.

Simplicity

We do not want people to think about how to use idemeum - we make it intutituve.

Security

We care about people's information - we secure our platform from the ground up.

Privacy

And we fundamentally believe that people need to be empowered to control their digital identity.

Change is needed

Most data breaches are caused by digital identity problems

Cyberattacks aren’t slowing down, and it’s worth noting that many attacks have been successful without the use of advanced technology. All it takes is one compromised credential or one legacy application to cause a data breach.

Over 80% of hacking breaches involve brute force or the use of lost or stolen credentials.

- Verizon 2020 DBIR

Up to 73% of users duplicate their passwords in both their personal and work accounts.

That is why

We built decentralized, verified, mobile, identity platform

We call it DVMI for short. As we care about people's privacy, we made idemeum decentralized. To ensure trust and security idemeum has identity verification built in. And to achieve frictionless user experience we designed idemeum to be mobile-first.

Decentralized

idemeum enables 100% privacy

We do not store any of the user identifiable information in our backend. Digital identity and all related claims are safely and securely stored on a user's mobile device. People can decide what to do with this information and who to share it with.

Verified

idemeum builds identity trust

Trust is fundamental in the online world. Especially in the new normal where everything is remote and virtual. We designed idemeum to verify person's digital identity and all associated claims. We make sure people are who they say they are. With the power of idemeum app everyone can decide what information they want to verify and add to idemeum profile.

Mobile

Convenience of a mobile device

idemeum offers an opportunity to always have your digital identity at your fingertips. When your mobile phone becomes your digital identity you can take it with you to any new job you have, you can use it at any online resource, and you can prove who you are without disclosing more information than you need. idemeum makes digital identity simple, convenient, and frictionless.
