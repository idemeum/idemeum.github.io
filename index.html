About our technology

idemeum connects to your HR system to automatically onboard employees without IT admin involvement. Ask an employee to install mobile app and self-onboard into your organization. No need to distribute passwords, no need to manually provision applications. We take care of that.
Do not worry about costly and time consuming password resets. Do not fret about breaches related to weak employee credentials and password re-use. We eliminate passwords. Employees use mobile devices to login to corporate applications with biometrics. And all this is power by Single Sign-On and MFA. Simple and secure.
Stop chasing your data and using separate point tools. You can get your application visibility in one place - see how applications are being used and how much you are spending on them as organization.
Make your employees more productive with mobile first experiences, such as SSO application catalog on a mobile device.
